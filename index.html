As an engineer with analytical experience, seeing the changes in the nature of work and the high volatility and uncertainty in today’s world, I decided to expand my horizons and follow a data science learning path. My focus has been in both the technical part with statistical analysis, visualization and programming, as well as non-technical aspects that are key to solving problems, including design thinking, storytelling, agile management and lean mindset.
